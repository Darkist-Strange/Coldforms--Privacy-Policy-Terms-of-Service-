# ColdForms Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2, 2026
**App Name:** ColdForms
**Owner/Company:** Jason Fehr
**Contact:** support@coldforms.io

## 1. Overview

ColdForms is an offline-first form programming and form-building application.

ColdForms is designed so that the forms, projects, responses, logic, charts, exports, and files you create in the app stay on your device. We do not have access to your local app content. We do not view, collect, sell, or analyze the forms, projects, responses, or files you create inside ColdForms.

The only information we may receive from you is information you choose to send to us, such as joining an email list or submitting a support request.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only limited information that you voluntarily provide.

### Email List Information

If you choose to join our email list, we may collect:

- Your email address
- Your name, if you choose to provide it
- Your communication preferences, if applicable

We use this information to send product updates, launch news, feature announcements, and related communications.

### Support Request Information

If you submit a support request, we may collect:

- Your email address
- Your name, if you choose to provide it
- The message or issue you submit
- Screenshots, files, logs, or other details you choose to include
- Basic technical information needed to understand or respond to the support request

Please do not include passwords, private customer records, confidential business information, sensitive personal information, or highly sensitive form responses in a support request unless it is necessary for us to help with the issue.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

ColdForms does not collect or transmit your local app content to us.

We do not collect:

- Forms you create
- Projects you save
- Form responses entered into your forms
- Local databases or saved project files
- Logic rules, formulas, graph structures, or runtime configurations
- Charts, dashboards, or analytics created locally
- Exported files, unless you choose to send them to us
- Files stored locally on your device
- Content you create, save, capture, or export inside the app

We cannot see what you make, save, capture, or export unless you intentionally send that information to us.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

- Respond to support requests
- Troubleshoot issues you report
- Send email updates if you joined our email list
- Improve ColdForms based on voluntary feedback
- Maintain support, security, legal, and business records
- Communicate with you about ColdForms when appropriate

We do not sell your personal information.

## 5. Local Data and User Content

ColdForms stores your app data locally on your device unless a feature clearly states otherwise or unless you choose to export, share, back up, or send data outside the app.

You are responsible for managing, protecting, exporting, backing up, and deleting your own local app data.

If you delete the app, reset your device, lose access to your device, or remove local files, your locally stored ColdForms data may be lost unless you have backed it up or exported it.

## 6. Support Submissions

If you contact support, you may choose to send us information about your issue. This may include text, screenshots, files, logs, or other details.

By submitting a support request, you agree that we may review and use the information you provide to respond to your request, troubleshoot the issue, and improve ColdForms.

You are responsible for deciding what information to include in a support request.

## 7. Email Communications

If you join the ColdForms email list, we may send you updates about the app, launch announcements, feature news, and related communications.

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us.

## 8. Third-Party Services

ColdForms uses a small number of third-party services for specific product functions.

- Apple App Store / App Store Connect, for app distribution, purchases, billing, refunds, and update delivery
- Supabase, for optional waitlist sign-ups, support ticket syncing, and support attachment storage when those cloud-backed features are used
- Sentry, for crash reporting and diagnostics in builds where crash reporting is enabled

These third-party services may process limited information needed to provide their services. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

ColdForms does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

The lightweight telemetry described in the app is stored locally on your device and is not sent to a third-party analytics provider unless this policy is updated.

If additional third-party processors are added later, this policy will be updated before those services are used beyond what applicable law allows.

## 9. Tracking and Advertising

ColdForms does not sell your personal information.

ColdForms does not use your local form content, project content, responses, exports, or files for advertising.

ColdForms does not track your activity across other companies’ apps or websites for advertising purposes unless this policy is updated and any required consent is obtained.

## 10. Data Retention

We keep email list information until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it.

We keep support request information for as long as needed to respond to you, maintain support records, improve the app, and meet legal or business obligations.

Local app data stored on your device is controlled by you and is not accessible to us.

## 11. Your Choices

You may:

- Unsubscribe from marketing emails
- Contact us to request deletion of your email list information
- Contact us to request deletion of support records associated with your email address, where legally and technically possible
- Choose what information to include in support requests
- Delete local ColdForms data from your device by using app features, deleting files, or removing the app

Because ColdForms is offline-first, we generally cannot access, recover, delete, or modify your local app data for you.

## 12. Children’s Privacy

ColdForms is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required in your region.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

## 13. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect the limited information we receive.

However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your own device, exported files, backups, passwords, and local project data.

## 14. International Users

ColdForms may be available to users in different regions. If you contact us, join an email list, or submit a support request, your information may be processed in the country where we or our service providers operate.

By using ColdForms or voluntarily submitting information to us, you understand that your information may be processed outside your region, subject to applicable law.

## 15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we update the policy, we will revise the effective date above. Material changes may be communicated through the app, our website, email, or other reasonable means.

## 16. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact us at:

**Jason Fehr**
**Email:** support@coldforms.io
